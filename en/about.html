📱 What is WooaQR?

WooaQR is a free online toolkit for QR code generation, reading, and barcode creation — all in your browser.

From URL, text, WiFi, contact, email, and phone QR codes to image-based and camera QR reading, CODE128/EAN-13/UPC-A barcode generation, and batch QR code creation — all in one place.

✅ Features

  • All processing happens in your browser — 100% safe
  • No files or data are sent to any server
  • Use instantly without signup or login
  • Supports URL, text, WiFi, contact, email, phone, and more QR formats
  • Real-time camera QR code scanning supported
  • Generate CODE128, EAN-13, UPC-A, CODE39 barcodes
  • Batch generate up to 20 QR codes and download as ZIP
  • Works conveniently on PC, tablet, and smartphone

🔒 Privacy Protected

All WooaQR features run entirely in your browser. No URL, text, or image data is sent to or stored on any server.

Camera QR scanning also runs locally via the browser API — no video data is transmitted externally.
